# Blinkit Sales & Outlet Performance Dashboard

An interactive, data-driven business intelligence dashboard designed to track, analyze, and optimize retail grocery sales performance across various outlet types, sizes, and geographic tiers for Blinkit (India's Last Minute App).

---

## Key Features & Visualizations

* **High-Level KPI Cards:** Instantly view vital metrics including Total Sales ($1.20M), Average Sales ($141), Number of Items (8,523), and Average Rating (3.9).
* **Outlet Establishment Trend:** A historical timeline tracking growth from 2012 to 2022, highlighting peak growth periods like the $205K high in 2018.
* **Outlet Size & Location Breakdown:**
  * **Size Analysis:** Visualizes market share split across Medium ($507.90K), Small ($444.79K), and High ($248.99K) business layouts.
  * **Location Tiers:** Analyzes geographic performance distributed across Tier 1 (336.40K), Tier 2 (393.15K), and Tier 3 (472.13K) areas.
* **Inventory & Product Analysis:**
  * **Fat Content Profile:** Monitors sales revenue splits between Low Fat ($776.32K) and Regular ($425.36K) products.
  * **Item Type Revenue:** A horizontal bar chart identifying top-performing categories such as Fruits & Vegetables ($0.18M) and Snack Foods ($0.18M) down to lower-volume categories like Seafood ($0.01M)[cite: 2].
* **Outlet Type Performance Matrix:** A comprehensive tabular breakdown assessing total sales, item visibility, and customer ratings across Grocery Stores, Supermarket Type 1, Supermarket Type 2, and Supermarket Type 3[cite: 2].
* **Interactive Slicers:** Dynamic top filters allowing users to drill down by Outlet Location Type, Outlet Size, and Item Type[cite: 2].

---

## Built With

* **Business Intelligence Tool:** Power BI 
* **Data Fields:** Retail sales logs, inventory fat content metrics, customer feedback logs, and location tier classifications[cite: 2].
